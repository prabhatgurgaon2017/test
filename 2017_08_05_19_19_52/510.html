Descriptive Information
Brief Title  ICMJE Registry of Pediatric Patients Diagnosed With Crohn's Disease in Belgium
Official Title  ICMJE Belgian Registry of Pediatric Crohn's Disease
Brief Summary A registry of pediatric patients (under 18 yrs of age) diagnosed with Crohn's disease was created in Belgium. Over a 2 year period 257 patients are included. These patients will be follow up prospectively during 5 years. Demographic data, family history, disease presentation and disease course including medication are registered in CRF's.
